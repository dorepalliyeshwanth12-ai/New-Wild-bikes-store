Reserve Your Machine

This is a demo payment page for your RetroVintage project. No real payments will be processed. After filling the form, you’ll be redirected to a Thank You page.

Step-by-step flow

  1. Select the bike you want to reserve and enter its Ref ID.
  2. Fill in your basic contact details and city.
  3. Choose a payment mode (UPI / Card / Cash on Delivery).
  4. Click on “Confirm Reservation” to go to the Thank You page.

You can later integrate real payment gateway / GA4 / GTM tracking on this page for your MENA cross-domain task.
